### Summary of this turn

__Multi-pass refinement is committed and working.__ 

The approach: after the dual-window trace, identify segments where x deviates from the rolling median by more than `multipass_drift_threshold` pixels, take medians of the clean context immediately before and after each suspicious segment, linearly interpolate a "predicted x" at every row, and snap each row to a dark pixel within ± `multipass_line_tolerance` of the predicted x (or mark as gap if no dark pixel near the prediction). Configurable via 5 new knobs in `DEFAULT_CONFIG` (`multipass_passes`, `multipass_drift_threshold`, `multipass_min_seg_len`, `multipass_line_tolerance`, `multipass_context_window`); set `multipass_passes=0` to disable.
